Both records occur at Prompt||GTFO in 2026, but the available evidence does not show whether other security educators are using AI-generated music similarly, whether Bar has published methodology, or whether “Cyber Vault Zero” influenced other awareness efforts.

Finally, the records do not document Bar’s broader professional background. They identify him as the author/presenter/performer, but they do not provide his role, affiliation, prior work, or other talks. Downstream researchers should avoid overgeneralizing from these two records.
